The Poetics of the Archive

Creative and Community Engagement with the Bloodaxe Archive

This is an interface to the catelogue and materials of the archive of Bloodaxe Books held at Newcastle University. It is in an early stage of development and is being made available so that we can gain comments, criticism and insight from visitors. The catelogue and digitisation process is continuing so a lot of information is missing or incomplete. Let us know what you think about the design here.
